The Problem

DeFi Exclusion & Currency Devaluation

Across Africa, individuals comfortable using a banking app are excluded from profitable DeFi opportunities. While they hold fiat savings that are eroded by inflation and devaluation, they lack accessible pathways to save and earn with DeFi strategies.

Barriers in Existing Crypto Onboarding Channels

Accessing crypto through centralized exchanges (CEXs) or peer-to-peer (P2P) platforms creates significant hurdles. High fees, fraud risks, complex onboarding, and reliance on third-party custody discourage both beginners and experienced users from participating confidently.

Overwhelming Complexity & Fragmentation in DeFi opportunities

Even after navigating the difficult on-ramps, users face a confusing and fragmented ecosystem. Profitable yield strategies are scattered across hundreds of protocols and multiple blockchains, with no clear way to discover, compare, or vet them. This complexity paralyzes new users, forcing them to either settle for low-yield options, give up entirely, or worse, fall for scams. The promise of DeFi remains out of reach.

The Solution

We are building

A self-custodial platform for on- and off-ramping, sending and receiving crypto, swapping between cryptocurrencies, and participating in interest-yielding DeFi strategies. Removing common onboarding barriers, such as the need to save seed phrases or hold native tokens for gas.

With our platform, users can:

On-ramp and off-ramp crypto directly to and from their local currencies (NGN, KES, ZAR).

Send stablecoins without needing a native token to pay gas fees( Gasless Transfer ).

Create a self-custody wallet instantly using Gmail.

Participate in yield-generating pools such as lending and DEX liquidity pools to passively earn interest

Take crypto-backed loans (using BTC, ETH, SOL, or other supported assets as collateral) and receive stablecoins instantly.

Seamlessly swap Stablecoins, Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other tokens across chains while avoiding unnecessary conversion fees.

Competitive Landscape

Our Unique Position in the Market.

Local Crypto Exchanges

(e.g., Luno, roqqu)

  • Weakness: Custodial platforms with limited features beyond basic trading and charges high fees. Users don't control their keys.
  • Our Edge: A self-custodial wallet created instantly with Gmail. We offer direct access to DeFi yield, low fee swaps, and crypto-backed loans.

P2P Marketplaces

(e.g., Bybit P2P)

  • Weakness: Riddled with fraud risk, scams, and high friction from slow dispute resolution and payment settlement.
  • Our Edge: A secure, non-custodial platform that eliminates counterparty risk and provides an instant, user-friendly on-ramp experience.

Traditional Remittance

(e.g., Western Union)

  • Weakness: Extremely high fees, slow settlement times (days), and poor currency exchange rates.
  • Our Edge: Near-instant, low-cost transactions where funds can be immediately deployed into interest-earning DeFi pools.
